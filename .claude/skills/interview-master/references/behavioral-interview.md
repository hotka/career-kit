# Behavioral Interview Guide

## STAR Method
- **Situation**: Set the context
- **Task**: Describe your responsibility
- **Action**: Explain what you did
- **Result**: Share the outcome

## Common Questions
1. Tell me about a time you faced a challenge
2. Describe a conflict with a team member
3. Tell me about a project you're proud of
4. How do you handle tight deadlines?
5. Describe a time you failed

## Preparation Tips
- Prepare 5-7 stories covering different scenarios
- Practice articulating them clearly
- Focus on your individual contribution
- Quantify results when possible
